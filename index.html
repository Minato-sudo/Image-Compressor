Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe to use for sensitive images?

Yes, absolutely. CompressIt processes all images entirely in your web browser. Your photos are never uploaded to any server or sent over the internet.

What is the maximum file size?

There is no hard limit, but we recommend keeping individual files under 25MB and batches under 30 images to ensure smooth performance in your browser.

Does it work offline?

Yes! Once you have loaded this page, you can disconnect from the internet and continue compressing images. Everything runs locally on your device.
